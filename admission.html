Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining our family.

What is the deadline for applications? expand_more
We accept applications on a rolling basis, but we recommend applying by March 15th for the upcoming September term to ensure space availability.
What are the age requirements for Kindergarten? expand_more
Children must be 5 years old by September 1st to be eligible for our Kindergarten program.
Do you offer financial aid or scholarships? expand_more
Yes, Hope Spring Foundation offers need-based financial assistance. Please request a financial aid packet from the admissions office after your initial tour.
